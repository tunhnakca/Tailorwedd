Who we are

Hey, we're Tailorwedd — tailors based in Istanbul. Our service is to professionally design a custom wedding dress and make it ready for use.

We bring our valued customers together with the most suitable wedding dresses and while doing this, we offer you a completely professional service. One more thing! We are aware of climate change and evaluate all stages accordingly. We offer you the products we use in recyclable form.

How it works

Your wedding dress is ready in 3 simple steps

01

Contact us

Just contact us and get your convenient appointment time! After that, all you have to do is wait for your appointment. Our team will prepare everything necessary for you.

02

Come to your appointment

Just drop by us at your special time! We've already prepared everything for you in advance. Your wedding dress will be prepared by our professional team in line with all your wishes.

03

Everything is ready

Your wedding dress is already prepared! When your wedding dress is ready, our team will contact you and deliver your wedding dress. Moreover, if your wedding dress does not fit on you as you want, our care service is free of charge.
